Who We Are . . .

Welcome to Aurea Vita. We're glad you're here.

We are Catalina and Liana, dreamers and stewards of Earth's most mesmerizing treasures. Our journey traverses the essence of Terra, the depth of Aqua, and the kaleidoscope of Flora: a tapestry woven with passion and reverence for the planet's wonders.

Based in the beautiful Pacific Northwest, our quest echoes the rhythmic dance of global biodiversity, curating a symphony of life's most captivating pieces exclusively for you. Each specimen, from the rare Siberian Fawn Lily to the ethereal Bohemian Moldevite, whispers a story, painting vibrant narratives that echo the soul of our Earth.

Join us on this voyage, as we invite you to infuse your space with the raw, untamed elegance of nature. Embrace the Earth's energy, let the waters speak to your spirit, and allow the lush greenery to weave its magic into the fabric of your home. Aurea Vita is not just a collection; it's an ode to the harmony between humanity and the natural world, beckoning you to create your own sanctuary of wonder and serenity.
